OUR SERVICES

We are experts in Advanced Total Oral Rehabilitation!

Dent House Services is a network of expert clinics that excel in Advanced Total Oral Rehabilitation through Dental Implants. Our brand is a point of reference in the national Dental Implantology market, since 2008

IMPLANTOLOGY

Implantology is the dentistry branch that recovers open spaces (without teeth) by inserting artificial dental “roots” that support a tooth prosthesis (crown). This happens by implanting a titanium screw into the bone tissue, where was tooth loss.

DENTAL AESTHETICS

Dental Aesthetics refers to the beauty of dentition and the dentist play a key role in this area, considering that all the procedures performed by them are intended to add, increase or restore the beauty of the tooth(s).

ENDODONTICS

It’s the branch of dentistry responsible for the study of dental pulp diseases and the associated processes. It encompasses the etiology, prevention, diagnosis and pulp treatment.

ORTHODONTICS

Orthodontics is the speciality related with the study, prevention and treatment of problems with growth, development and maturation of the face, dental arcades and bite, in other words, of the dentofacial dysfunctions.

PAEDIATRIC DENTISTRY

It’s the branch of dentistery that is dedicated to the oral health of babies, children and adolescents. This area aims to preserve healthy deciduous dentition (or milk), because this is important not only for the facial development of the growing child but also for the development of permanent (definitive) dentition.

3D X-RAY

Our clinic is prepared with 3D X-Ray equipment that performs Digital Orthopantomographies and also Computed Axial Tomographies (CAT), which makes the diagnostic service faster and facilitates our internal service. Also available in external services.
